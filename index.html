CS Website Prettifier

Prettify! ‚Üê Drag the "Prettify!" tag below to your browser's Bookmark tab, and then click it when browsing some of the UCLA CS plain websites.

Turning on the bookmark tab:

  • Chrome: use ctrl+shift+B (command+shift+B on a Mac) to toggle the Bookmark Toolbar.
  • Firefox: right-click in the blank space of the tab bar and check "Bookmark Toolbar".
  • Safari: command+shift+B to toggle the Bookmark Toolbar.

This prettifier works for most of the UCLA CS webpages written in pure (plain) HTML.
